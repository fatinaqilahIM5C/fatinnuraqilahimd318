My Personal Experience ✨

Internship at PPAP

I completed my internship at Perbadanan Perpustakaan Awam Pahang (PPAP), where I was exposed to real working environments in a public library setting. I learned how daily operations are managed and gained hands-on experience.

Visiting Museum Sungai Lembing

During my internship, I had the opportunity to visit the Sungai Lembing Museum with other trainees, which provided insight into the town’s rich tin mining history. The museum showcased mining artifacts and explained the lives of miners, helping us understand the area’s historical and economic significance

Playing Tourist

My friends and I went to Pekan, Pahang, to explore the town like tourists. We visited local attractions, enjoyed the scenic spots, and experienced the culture and history of the area. It was a fun and relaxing trip that allowed us to bond while discovering new places.

Merdeka Fireworks at KLCC

I watched the Merdeka fireworks display at KLCC, which was a spectacular celebration of Malaysia’s independence. The sky lit up with vibrant colors, synchronized beautifully with music, creating an unforgettable atmosphere. Being there with the crowd, I felt a strong sense of national pride and excitement.

SPM Art Project

For my SPM Art Project, I created a large book cover with the theme of a yoyo, inspired by old games. I incorporated nostalgic elements from classic video games into the design to make it visually engaging. The project allowed me to explore creativity while combining artistic skills with a playful, hands-on concept.

MY First Spa Day

I went for a facial treatment during a spa day, and I enjoyed it so much that it has now become a monthly commitment. The experience helped me unwind and take care of my skin, making self-care a regular part of my routine. It has since become a personal ritual that I look forward to every month.
